The little house with the big welcome

Little Guest House has been looking after travellers from its corner near Manee Nopparat Road for years — a family place with sixteen air-conditioned rooms, some with sitting areas and kitchenettes.

Guests come for the price and stay for the people: the reviews repeat the same words — clean, comfortable, helpful, kind. Wat Chiang Man is around the corner and Wat Chedi Luang a thirteen-minute walk.
