The idea

More of a flat than a room

Windsor Garden House is a Lanna-style house wrapped around a garden, tucked on a quiet soi right behind Chiang Mai University. Inside, the rooms aren't really rooms — they're small apartments, with a kitchen corner, a dining table, a fridge and a proper bathroom of your own.

It's the kind of place that suits a fortnight better than a night: cook when you want, spread out, come and go. Nimman is a short walk, the old city a short ride, and the whole thing costs a fraction of a hotel.
