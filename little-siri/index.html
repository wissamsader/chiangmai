The house

A home, not a hotel

The Little Siri is a converted teak-floored townhouse on Siri Mangkalajarn Soi 5 — the quiet, leafy street that runs parallel to Nimman. Downstairs there’s one long room to live in: a work counter with bar stools, a coffee corner, a guitar on the sofa, and more plants than furniture.

Upstairs, sunny rooms with soundproofed windows open onto plant-lined balconies. It’s properly pet-friendly — the house dog usually gets to the door before the humans do — and Nimman’s cafés and galleries start five minutes down the soi.
