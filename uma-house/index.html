The house

A house you disappear into the green of — reached by wooden walkways through a living garden.

Uma House sits low under the canopy in Suthep, where raised teak decks thread between the rooms through dense, breathing foliage. The rooms are calm — deep blue walls, bamboo ceilings, carved headboards — the kind of quiet you feel in your shoulders.

There's a pool under the trees, a shared kitchen, and birdsong instead of traffic. Baan Kang Wat art village and the meditation temple at Wat Ram Poeng are a five-minute walk — which is why so many "one week" stays quietly become three.
