The house

The quiet corner, redrawn

Baan Pisamai is a small new-build guesthouse tucked behind Mani Nopparat Road, where the Old City moat turns its north-east corner. Everything still feels out of the wrapper: white rooms with black-steel windows, proper desks, kettles and fridges, and rain-shower bathrooms that shine.

The location is a walker’s trick — Chang Puak Gate one way, Tha Phae Gate the other, and Warorot Market’s morning chaos thirteen minutes on foot. Twenty-eight guests have scored it so far; the average is 4.9.
