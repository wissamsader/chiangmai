Welcome

The golden thread of the Old City

Saithong — “golden thread” in Thai — is a small guesthouse on a soi off Rachadamnoen Road, the Old City’s main artery. Ten simple rooms with balconies and sitting corners look out over the rooftops of Soi 3.

Location is the luxury here: Wat Phra Singh at one end of the street, Tha Phae Gate at the other, and on Sunday evenings the Walking Street market unrolls right past your soi. More than a thousand travellers have reviewed the house across Google and Agoda.
