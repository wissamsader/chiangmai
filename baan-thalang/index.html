Welcome

A garden house north of the walls

Baan Thalang is a small guesthouse on Hussadhisawee Road, a short walk north of Chiang Mai’s Old City. Rooms open onto balconies with mountain views; downstairs there’s a serene garden and a family-run restaurant.

Guests come back for the calm — and for the hosts, who arrange bikes, cars and tours without any fuss. Chang Puak Market is under a kilometre away; Wat Phra Singh is a sixteen-minute walk.
