The house

โอบอุ่น means "a warm embrace" — and it's not just a name

Ob-Un is a small family homestay behind a leafy gate in Suthep, where the lane runs quiet beside the Chonprathan canal. Teak floors, art on the walls, a garden you'll want to sit in, and OON Café right out front for your first coffee of the day.

Tap and Nui run it themselves. Nui's homemade breakfast is the reason people write that they felt at home — and the reason a lot of one-night bookings quietly turn into a week.
